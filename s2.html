FOOD

Bon Appétit little scientists!





Today, we're putting on our lab coats and diving into the delicious
world of "The Food We Eat."
Our bodies are like super machines, and the food we eat is like the fuel that keeps us going strong. Let's explore this tasty science adventure together!

1. Meet the Food Pals:
Imagine your favorite foods – juicy fruits, crunchy vegetables, and yummy snacks.

These are your food pals! They come in all shapes, sizes, and colors, and each one brings something special to the table.

2. The Superpowers of Fruits:
Fruits are like nature's candies! They're packed with vitamins and give us the energy to play, run, and have lots of fun.

From apples to bananas, each fruit has its own superpowers.

3. The Crunchy Veggie Heroes:
Veggies are the heroes of our meals. They make our bodies strong and help us see in the dark (like carrots do!).

Broccoli, spinach, and peas – they're the crunchy, green superheroes.

4. The Energy-Boosting Grains:
Bread, rice, and pasta are grains that give us super energy.

They're like the fuel that helps us run, jump, and play all day long. Let's thank our grainy friends for their energy-boosting magic!

5. The Dairy Crew:
Milk, cheese, and yogurt are part of the dairy crew.

They make our bones strong and help us grow tall. Say "cheese" for strong bones and healthy smiles!

6. Water – The Hydration Hero:
Don't forget water! It's like a superhero drink that keeps us hydrated.

Water helps our bodies stay cool, and it's perfect for quenching our thirst after a day of adventures.

7. The Sweet Treats:
Who doesn't love a sweet treat now and then?

Cookies, cakes, and ice cream are like special desserts that we enjoy in moderation. They add a sprinkle of happiness to our lives!
