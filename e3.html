Pronouns

What are your Pronouns??





Today, we're stepping into the enchanting realm of "Pronouns" –
the magical word helpers that make our sentences smoother and more exciting.
Get ready to discover the wonders of pronouns!

1. Meet the Pronoun Wizards:
Pronouns are like magical word wizards that help us avoid repeating the same names over and over in our sentences.

They make our language flow smoothly and add a touch of mystery to our stories.

2. Personal Pronouns - Our Special Names:
First up, we have personal pronouns – the special names for people! Instead of saying "Sarah" or "Sam" every time, we use words like "he," "she," or "they."

These pronouns keep our sentences neat and tidy.

3. Demonstrative Pronouns - Pointing the Way:
Next, let's explore demonstrative pronouns – the words that help us point to things.

"This," "that," "these," and "those" are like magic pointers that show exactly what we're talking about. They add a dash of mystery to our stories!

4. Possessive Pronouns - Our Own Magical Possessions:
Now, let's unveil possessive pronouns – the magical words that show what belongs to whom. Instead of saying "Sarah's toy" every time, we use "her toy."

Possessive pronouns add a sprinkle of ownership to our language.

5. Reflexive Pronouns - Mirror Magic: Reflexive pronouns are like mirror magic! When someone does something to themselves, we use words like "myself," "yourself," and "himself."

They add a twist of self-action to our sentences.
