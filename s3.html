SEASON

Cuz it's HOT and it's COLD!





Today, let's embark on an exciting journey through the wonderful world of "Seasons."
Just like a magical dance, nature changes its outfit four times a year,
and each season brings its own special charm. Get ready to explore the fascinating cycle of the seasons!

1. Spring - Nature's Awakening:

As winter bids farewell, flowers bloom, trees sprout new leaves, and colorful butterflies flutter around.

Spring is like a giant garden party, and everyone is invited to celebrate the rebirth of nature.

2. Summer - Sunshine and Fun:
When the sun shines the brightest, it's summertime!

Days are longer, and the air is warm. It's the perfect season for picnics, ice cream cones, and playing in the sunshine. Summer is like a big, happy playground for everyone to enjoy!

3. Autumn - The Cozy Harvest:
As summer cools down, autumn arrives with a burst of warm colors. Trees put on their golden, red, and orange costumes, and the air gets a little crisp.

It's time for cozy sweaters, jumping in leaf piles, and enjoying the bounty of the harvest. Autumn is nature's way of preparing for a restful sleep.

4. Winter - Frosty Wonderland:
When snowflakes start to twirl from the sky, winter has arrived!

Everything turns into a sparkling, frosty wonderland. It's time for snowball fights, building snowmen, and sipping hot cocoa by the fireplace. Winter is like a magical storybook come to life.
