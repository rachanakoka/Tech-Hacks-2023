Shapes

The names to your drawings!




Welcome to our exciting shapes adventure!
Today, we are going to discover the amazing world of shapes. Shapes are all around us, and they help us understand and describe the things we see.

What Are Shapes?
Shapes are like the building blocks of everything! They are the different forms
that objects can have. Let's get to know some special shapes:

Circle: A circle is round, like a delicious pizza or the sun in the sky. Can you think of something else that's shaped like a circle?

Square: A square has four equal sides, just like a perfect sandwich or a picture frame. Look around—can you find anything square in your room?

Triangle: A triangle has three sides, like a slice of pizza or the roof of a house. Triangles are super cool!

Rectangle: A rectangle is like a stretched-out square. It has two long sides and two short sides, just like a book or a door. What else can you find that's shaped like a rectangle?

Let's Find Shapes!
Now, let's go on a shape hunt! Look around your room or your house. Can you find circles, squares, triangles, and rectangles? Count how many you see!

Shape Art Time
Grab your crayons and paper—it's time to create! Draw a big circle, a square house, a triangle tree, or a rectangle door. Use your imagination and make your shapes colorful!

Sorting Shapes
Let's sort some objects by their shapes! Grab a few toys or items from around your house. Can you sort them into groups of circles, squares, triangles, and rectangles?

Shape Challenge
Your shape challenge is to find the most unique or interesting shape in your house. It could be a special toy, a picture frame, or even a piece of furniture. Describe it to your friends or family!
